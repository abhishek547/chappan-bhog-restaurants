OUR PHILOSPHY
FRESH. FOOD. NEARLY.

Sometimes we are very hungry and have little time. Or just crazy about fast food. And that's okay too. But why shouldn't a quick snack be fresh anyway? "Fast-Food" - for us that means first and foremost that you get your food quickly. Everything else in the Frittenwerk is not reminiscent of a snack bar. Look forward to fast, fresh, varied food at fair prices in a great atmosphere.

Our House Fries

Anyone who doesn't just call themselves “snack bar” but rather “French fries manufacturer” either puts on a lot of fat or has damn good fries. We give everything every day to convince you of the second. What is really quite thick, on the other hand, are our fries themselves: You can tell from our special housecut that this is not a frozen product. For this we only use the best potatoes with a unique taste. This makes our fries crispy on the outside and soft on the inside.

HOMEMADE SAUCES & DIPS

If a french fry is really crispy, you can dip it in a creamy dip with a clear conscience. We offer you a large selection of different sauces based on your own home recipe. From the fresh lemon aioli to the original Frittenwerk ketchup to the warm gravy, there is something for every taste. Many of our sauces are even vegan.

100% VEGETABLE OIL

We only use 100% vegetable oil. This is good news not only for vegetarians but also for taste lovers. Because only vegetable oil ensures a balanced, consistently delicious French fries taste - without the oil's own strong flavor.

ENVIRONMENTALLY FRIENDLY PACKAGING - 100% BIODEGRADABLE

Our snack offer is not only rich, but also sustainable. We use cutlery, french fries boats and carrier bags made from original materials that are completely biodegradable. Our packaging disappears by itself soon after your visit - even if not quite as quickly as our fries.

EAT WELL. SAVE NATURE

The difference starts with you! With your visit to the Frittenwerk you are already doing your part to an environmentally conscious eating culture. Because sustainability and environmental protection are just as important to us as they are to you, we do without plastic products at all ends. In this way, we not only avoid a lot of harmful plastic waste, but also save water and oil. More information: www.frittenwerk.com/gogreen
